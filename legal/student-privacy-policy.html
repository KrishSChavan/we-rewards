Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 19, 2026  ·  Last Updated: July 19, 2026

Summary WeRewards collects your Google account info, point transaction history, and engagement data to run the loyalty program. We do not sell your data. You can export or delete your data at any time through the app.

1. Who We Are

WeRewards ("we," "us," "our") is a loyalty rewards platform operated by Krish Chavan, an individual based in Pennsylvania. WeRewards is an independent service and is not affiliated with The Pennsylvania State University.

For privacy-related questions, contact us at: contactwerewards@gmail.com

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide (via Google Sign-In)

Signing in with Google does not by itself create a WeRewards account. After you choose your Google account we ask you to accept the Terms and this Policy, and your account is created only when you do. If you decline, the sign-in is discarded and nothing about you is kept.

Once you accept, we store:

  • Your full name (from your Google profile)
  • Your email address
  • A unique user identifier assigned by our authentication provider (Supabase)

Your Google profile photo URL is displayed in the app but is fetched directly from Google's servers and is not stored in our database.

2.2 Transaction and Balance Data

Each time you earn or redeem points at a vendor, we record:

  • The vendor involved
  • The type of transaction (earn or redeem)
  • The number of points earned or redeemed
  • The dollar amount of the purchase (for earn transactions)
  • The reward redeemed (for redeem transactions)
  • The date and time of the transaction

We also maintain your current point balance at each participating vendor.

2.3 Engagement Score Data

We compute a rolling 30-day engagement score that determines your earn multiplier tier. This score is derived from your transaction history and reflects:

  • How many different vendors you visit (breadth)
  • How often you return to the same vendors (loyalty)
  • Your spending volume across vendors (spend)

This score is stored and updated automatically.

2.4 Short-Lived Codes

We generate temporary earn codes (6-digit) and redemption codes (4-digit) for your account. Redemption codes are single-use and expire 120 seconds after you generate them. Earn codes expire 5 minutes after they are issued; while you have the app open, your existing code is refreshed rather than replaced, so the same 6 digits may stay valid for as long as you keep the screen open. Both are automatically deleted after use or expiration.

2.5 Error and Diagnostic Logs

If the app encounters an error, we may automatically record a crash report containing your user ID, the page you were on (path only — never the query string), the HTTP method and response status of the failed request, a technical error message and stack trace, your browser's user agent string, and a small amount of technical context about what the app was doing at the time. Error logs are retained for 90 days and then automatically deleted.

2.6 Consent Records

When you accept the Terms and this Policy after signing in, we record that you did so: your user identifier, which version of the documents you accepted, the date and time, and — as evidence that the acceptance was genuine — your IP address and browser user agent at that moment.

This is the only place we record your IP address. We keep these records for as long as your account exists; they are deleted with it. If we revise the documents and ask you to accept again, that creates a new record rather than replacing the old one, so the history of what you agreed to and when stays intact.

2.7 Information Stored on Your Device

WeRewards does not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or any third-party analytics. We do store the following in your browser's local storage:

  • Your sign-in session. When you sign in with Google, our authentication provider (Supabase) stores an access token and a refresh token in your browser so you stay signed in between visits. These are what keep you logged in; clearing your browser storage signs you out.
  • Your appearance preference. Your light/dark theme choice is saved locally so the app opens the way you left it.
  • An offline app cache. The app registers a service worker that caches its own pages and styles so it loads quickly and works on a poor connection. This cache holds application files, not your personal data.

All three are stored on your device rather than sent to us as a separate collection of data. You can clear them at any time through your browser's site-data settings.

2.8 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Payment card numbers or bank account information
  • Precise GPS location
  • Penn State student ID numbers or academic records
  • Biometric data
  • Contacts, photos, or files from your device

6. Account Deletion and Data Removal

You may delete your WeRewards account at any time through the Account tab → Delete my account. When you delete your account:

  • Your profile (name, email, user ID) is permanently deleted.
  • Your point balances and engagement scores are permanently deleted.
  • Your active earn and redemption codes are permanently deleted.
  • Your consent records, including the IP address recorded at acceptance, are permanently deleted.
  • Your transaction history records are retained but permanently anonymized — the link between those records and your identity is severed. This is necessary to preserve the accuracy of vendors' business records (e.g., total revenue statistics) which we are contractually obligated to maintain.

One exception. If the app recorded a diagnostic error log for your account before you deleted it (see Section 2.5), that log keeps your user identifier until it is purged on its normal 90-day schedule. These logs contain no name, email, or transaction data — only the identifier and technical debugging fields. After 90 days, no identifier associated with your account remains anywhere in our systems.

Deletion is irreversible. Once deleted, your account and points cannot be recovered.

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Data Export

You can download a full copy of your WeRewards data (profile, balances, full transaction history, and engagement scores) at any time through Account tab → Download my data. The file is provided as a JSON file.

7.2 Account Deletion

As described in Section 6, you may delete your account at any time through the app.

7.3 Correction

If you believe your account information is inaccurate, contact us at contactwerewards@gmail.com.

7.4 Opt-Out of Communications

We do not currently send marketing emails or push notifications to students. If this changes, we will update this Policy and provide opt-out mechanisms.

8. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS), server-side authentication token validation, and database access controls. No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Earn codes and redemption codes are short-lived and single-use, which limits the impact of code interception.

9. Pennsylvania Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information (as defined under Pennsylvania's Breach of Personal Information Notification Act, 73 P.S. §§ 2301–2329), we will notify you without unreasonable delay and in accordance with applicable law. If a breach affects 500 or more Pennsylvania residents, we will also notify the Pennsylvania Attorney General's Office as required.

10. Children's Privacy

WeRewards is not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, contact us at contactwerewards@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.

12. Vendor Applicants and Vendor Contacts

This section applies to businesses applying to join WeRewards, not to students. If you are a student, Sections 1–11 cover you and this section does not.

12.1 What We Collect from Applicants

When a business submits the WeRewards vendor application form, we collect: the business name, a contact person's name, a contact phone number, a contact email address, a password chosen for the future vendor terminal login, and optionally a street address, a business logo image, and a free-text message.

Passwords are stored only as a bcrypt hash — we never store or have access to the password itself.

12.2 How We Use It

Application information is used solely to evaluate the application, contact the applicant, and — if the application is approved — create and operate the vendor's terminal account. We do not sell vendor contact information or use it for marketing unrelated to WeRewards.

12.3 Address Geocoding

If a business address is provided, we send that address to Nominatim, a geocoding service run by the OpenStreetMap Foundation, to convert it into map coordinates for display in the student app. Only the business address is sent.

12.4 Retention

Applications are retained while under review and, if approved, for as long as the vendor account is active. Declined and withdrawn applications are retained only as long as needed for our records. A vendor's own obligations and data rights are governed by the vendor agreement signed with WeRewards.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The current version is always available in the app and at /legal/student-privacy-policy.html, and the "Last Updated" date at the top reflects the latest revision.

For material changes we will prompt you to review and accept the revised Policy the next time you open the app, and record that acceptance as described in Section 2.6. For minor changes, your continued use after the revision is posted constitutes acceptance.
