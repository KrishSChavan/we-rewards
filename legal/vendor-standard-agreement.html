This Standard Vendor Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of the Agreement Date above by and between Krish Chavan, operating as WeRewards ("Company"), and the Vendor identified above ("Vendor"). Together, the Parties agree as follows:

1. Definitions

  • "Platform" means the WeRewards software application, including the vendor terminal, the student-facing app, the administrative dashboard, and all associated infrastructure.
  • "Terminal Device" or "Device" means the electronic device provided by Company to Vendor for use with the Platform's vendor terminal backend.
  • "Monthly Fee" means the flat monthly subscription fee set forth in the Key Terms above.
  • "Subscription Period" means each calendar month during which this Agreement is in effect and a Monthly Fee is due.
  • "Points" means the loyalty points awarded to students through the Platform in connection with qualifying purchases at Vendor's location.

2. Term

This Agreement begins on the Agreement Date and continues on a month-to-month basis until terminated by either Party in accordance with Section 11 (Termination).

3. Platform Services

Subject to payment of the Monthly Fee and compliance with this Agreement, Company grants Vendor a limited, non-exclusive, non-transferable license to use the WeRewards Platform during the term of this Agreement to:

  • Award loyalty points to students by entering student earn codes into the vendor terminal;
  • Manage and publish your business's reward offerings through the terminal's ITEMS interface;
  • Accept student point redemptions at your location through the terminal's REDEEM interface;
  • View transaction history, customer engagement analytics, and revenue summaries through the terminal's STATS interface;
  • Configure your business profile (points-per-dollar rate, quick-amount buttons, address, logo) through the terminal's SETTINGS interface.

Company reserves the right to modify Platform features at any time. Material reductions in core functionality will be communicated to Vendor with reasonable advance notice.

4. Equipment Loan

4.1 Loan of Device

Company will provide Vendor with one (1) Terminal Device (an electronic device connected to the WeRewards terminal backend). The Device is loaned to Vendor with the Monthly Fee covering software access only; no separate hardware fee is charged. The Device remains the sole property of Company at all times. This arrangement constitutes a bailment, not a sale or lease.

4.2 Vendor Responsibilities

Vendor agrees to:

  • Use the Device solely for operating the WeRewards vendor terminal at the Vendor's listed business location;
  • Keep the Device in good working condition, free from physical damage caused by misuse or negligence;
  • Maintain a stable internet connection at the business location sufficient to operate the terminal;
  • Not install unauthorized software on the Device, modify the Device's firmware, or attempt to access systems beyond the WeRewards terminal;
  • Promptly notify Company if the Device is lost, stolen, or damaged;
  • Not transfer, sublease, or make the Device available to any third party without Company's prior written consent.

4.3 Risk of Loss and Damage

Vendor bears the risk of loss, theft, or damage to the Device from the time of delivery. If the Device is lost, stolen, or damaged beyond normal wear and tear, Vendor agrees to pay Company a replacement fee of $        , representing Company's reasonable cost to replace the Device.

4.4 Return of Device

Upon termination of this Agreement for any reason, Vendor must return the Device to Company in good working condition (normal wear and tear excepted) within 10 calendar days of the termination date. Company may remotely wipe the Device upon termination. Failure to return the Device within 10 days entitles Company to charge Vendor the replacement fee described in Section 4.3.

4.5 Device Condition at Handoff

Company and Vendor will document the Device's condition at delivery (e.g., photographs, signed receipt). This record will serve as the baseline for any damage claims.

5. Fees and Payment

5.1 Monthly Fee

Vendor agrees to pay the Monthly Fee set forth in the Key Terms above. Fees are billed monthly, in advance, beginning on the First Billing Date. Payment is due within 15 days of the invoice date.

5.2 Late Payment

Accounts more than 30 days past due may be suspended until payment is received. Accounts more than 45 days past due may be terminated by Company. Company reserves the right to charge a late fee of 1.5% per month on overdue balances, to the extent permitted by applicable law.

5.3 Fee Changes

Company may change the Monthly Fee by providing Vendor with at least 30 days' written notice before the new fee takes effect. If Vendor does not wish to accept the new fee, Vendor may terminate this Agreement without penalty before the new fee takes effect.

5.4 No Refunds

Monthly Fees are non-refundable. If Vendor terminates this Agreement mid-month, no refund is issued for the remainder of that month.

6. Vendor Obligations

  • Accurate Transactions: Vendor agrees to enter accurate dollar amounts when awarding points to students. Vendor is responsible for any points fraudulently or erroneously awarded through the terminal on Vendor's account.
  • PIN Security: Vendor must set a terminal PIN before accepting any point redemptions. Redeem and settings features are gated by this PIN, and until one is set they are not protected. Vendor is responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of the PIN, for limiting it to authorized staff, and for changing it immediately if Vendor believes it has been compromised.
  • Reward Fulfillment: Vendor agrees to honor all active rewards listed on its terminal. If Vendor cannot fulfill a reward, it must deactivate that reward through the ITEMS interface before accepting redemption codes for it.
  • Compliance with Law: Vendor agrees to operate the WeRewards program in compliance with all applicable local, state, and federal laws.
  • Authorized Use Only: Vendor agrees not to use the Platform to defraud students, award false points, or manipulate the loyalty program in a manner inconsistent with genuine commercial transactions.
  • Internet Connectivity: Vendor is responsible for maintaining internet service at its location sufficient to operate the terminal. Company is not responsible for downtime caused by connectivity issues at Vendor's location.
  • Notification of Changes: Vendor agrees to promptly notify Company of any change to its business name, address, or contact information.

7. Company Obligations

  • Provide access to the Platform in substantial accordance with its current functionality while this Agreement is in effect;
  • Make reasonable efforts to maintain Platform availability, without guaranteeing 100% uptime;
  • Provide reasonable support for Platform issues via email;
  • Provide Vendor with access to its transaction analytics through the terminal's STATS interface.

Company does not guarantee any particular number of student users, transaction volume, or revenue outcome for Vendor.

8. Platform Rules and Limits

  • The maximum single earn transaction is $200 (enforced by the Platform).
  • Void/reversal of a transaction is only available within 1 minute of the original transaction.
  • Vendor may set its own points-per-dollar rate within Platform-enforced limits (0.5 – 1,000 points per dollar).
  • Company may implement additional platform rules from time to time with reasonable notice to Vendor.

9. Data and Privacy

9.1 Student Data

Vendor acknowledges that WeRewards collects personal information from students as described in the WeRewards Student Privacy Policy. Vendor agrees not to request, record, or use student personal data beyond what is displayed on the terminal during a legitimate transaction.

9.2 Vendor Business Data

Company retains all transaction records processed through the Platform. Vendor's business analytics are accessible through the terminal's STATS interface. Company may use aggregated, anonymized transaction data across the Platform for analytics or platform improvement purposes.

9.3 Vendor Application Data

Information submitted through the vendor application form is used solely to set up and manage Vendor's account. Company will not sell Vendor's contact information to third parties.

9.4 Data on Termination

Upon termination and hard-deletion of a vendor account, transaction records are anonymized (vendor identifier removed) but not deleted, to preserve historical integrity. Vendor's profile, staff, rewards, and session data are permanently deleted.

All software, design, branding, and infrastructure comprising the WeRewards Platform are owned by or licensed to Krish Chavan. Vendor is granted only the limited license described in Section 3. Nothing in this Agreement transfers any intellectual property rights to Vendor.

Vendor retains ownership of its own business name, logo, and brand. By providing a logo for display on the Platform, Vendor grants Company a non-exclusive license to display that logo solely for identifying the Vendor within the WeRewards app.

11. Termination

11.1 Termination by Vendor

Vendor may terminate this Agreement at any time by providing written notice to Company at contactwerewards@gmail.com. Vendor is responsible for subscription fees accrued through the end of the current billing month. No refunds are issued for partial months.

11.2 Termination by Company

Company may terminate this Agreement immediately, without penalty, if Vendor:

  • Materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure the breach within 10 days of written notice;
  • Engages in fraud, abuse of the Platform, or conduct harmful to students or Company's reputation;
  • Fails to pay any Monthly Fee within 45 days of the due date.

Company may also terminate this Agreement without cause by providing 30 days' written notice to Vendor.

11.3 Effect of Termination

Upon termination, Vendor's Platform access is revoked, the Terminal Device must be returned per Section 4.4, and students' unredeemed points associated with the Vendor will no longer be redeemable. Company is not liable for any losses resulting from termination.

11.4 Suspension

Company may temporarily suspend Vendor's Platform access for investigation of suspected fraud, abuse, or non-payment, and will notify Vendor of the suspension as promptly as reasonably possible.
