How your multiplier works

Your points multiplier climbs the more you use WeRewards over the last 30 days. You earn a score from 0–1000 that blends three things:

  • Variety — how many different spots you visit
  • Loyalty — how often you come back to the same spots
  • Spend — how much you spend, with extra weight for meal-sized orders

Keeping all three balanced climbs fastest. Reach 350 for 2× points, or 700 for 3× — the multiplier applies to everything you earn. It's a rolling 30-day window, so keep visiting to hold your tier.
